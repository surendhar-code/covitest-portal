STEPS TO BE FOLLOWED DURING QUARANTINE PERIODS


QUARANTINE DURATION

The home quarantine period is for 14 days from contact with a confirmed case or earlier if suspect case (of whom the index person is a contact) turns out negative on Covid test.

SELF ISOLATE

Stay in a well-ventilated single-room preferably with an attached/separate toilet. Needs to stay away from elderly people, pregnant women, children and persons with co-morbidities within the household. Restrict his/her movement within the house. Under no circumstances attend any social/religious gathering.

HEALTH INSTRUCTIONS

Wash hand as often thoroughly with soap and water or with alcohol-based hand sanitizer. Avoid sharing household items e.g. dishes, drinking glasses or other items with other people at home. Wear a surgical mask at all the time. The mask should be changed every 6-8 hours and disposedoff. Disposable masks are never to be reused.

FAMILY MEMBERS INSTRUCTIONS

Only an assigned family member should be tasked with taking care of the such person. Avoid shaking the soiled linen or direct contact with skin. Use disposable gloves when cleaning the surfaces or handling soiled linen Wash hands after removing gloves. Visitors should not be allowed. In case the person being quarantined becomes symptomatic, all his close contacts will be home quarantined (for 14 days) and followed up for an additional 14days or till the report of such case turns out negative on Covitest portal.

ENVIRONMENT SANITATION

Clean and disinfect frequently touched surfaces in the quarantined personâ€™s room (e.g. bed frames, tables etc.) daily with 1% Sodium Hypochlorite Solution. Clean and disinfect toilet surfaces daily with regular household bleach solution/phenolic disinfectants. Clean the clothes and other linen used by the person separately using common household detergent and dry.

REGULAR MONITORING

It is important to monitor yourself and check on your recovery progress and statistics throughout the isolation period. Use oximeter and thermometer to check oxygen level and temperature respectively. Maintain record of all observations .eat nutritious meals and stay hydrated.

MEDICATIONS

It is advised to resort to supportive medicines such as cough syrups and to help any mild symptoms of the virus. Always consult a doctor or an expert before implementing any drastic medical changes to your recovery and self isolation routine.

SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION

Medical attention is necessary regardless of the severity of the infection. Stay in touch with a doctor through phone or video calls to help check for diagnosis and recovery progress reports. However, in the case where the infection is between moderate to severe, contact a doctor immediately. If you are having difficulty in breathing, persistent pain, a fever of 101 degrees celsius or above or are finding it difficult to wake up or stay awake, it is advised that you contact a professional caretaker.

STAY CALM

Stay relaxed and focused as soon as you test positive for COVID. Most of the scenarios and possibilities that you would overthink about may not even occur which means that you may have spent unnecessary time stressing about hypothetical situations which could adversely affect your recovery process. Maintaining a stable and calm state of mind is vital during the isolation and recovery phase.The entire duration of the infection may be as stressful or as stress free as you allow it to be. The true key is to stay composed and take the necessary actions as required and prescribed by a doctor or a professional.
